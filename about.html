About Digambara Industries

Delivering reliable Diesel Exhaust Fluid solutions with quality and compliance.

DIGAMBARA INDUSTRIES is a premier manufacturer specializing in Diesel Exhaust Fluid (DEF), a crucial component for modern diesel engines in order to meet stringent emission standards.

Founded in 2006 in Pune as a proprietorship firm, we have quickly established ourselves in the industry and have obtained certifications for compliance with ISO ISO 45001: 2018 and as per ISO 22241, reflecting our commitment to maintaining high-quality production standards.

Our DEF is offered in a variety of packaging options, including 5L, 10L, 20L, 50L, 200L, and 1000L containers, ensuring we meet the specific requirements of different sectors, from small businesses to large industrial clients.

Our dedicated team of over 10+ skilled professionals utilizes cutting-edge technology and advanced manufacturing processes to produce DEF. This enables us to support modern diesel vehicles in adhering to evolving emission regulations, thereby promoting cleaner air and a more sustainable future
