Diesel Exhaust Fluid (DEF)

High purity Diesel Exhaust Fluid manufactured in compliance with ISO 22241 standards for optimal Selective Catalytic Reduction (SCR) performance.

Key Benefits
  • ✔ Environment Friendly
  • ✔ Reduces NOx Pollution
  • ✔ Improves Fuel Economy
  • ✔ Ensures SCR System Efficiency
  • ✔ Extends SCR System Lifespan
Applications

Essential for vehicles equipped with SCR technology to reduce emissions and maintain environmental compliance.

Important Storage Guidelines

Store below 30°C & above 11°C
Protect from contamination (fuel, oil, dust, metals)
Use dedicated storage & dispensing equipment
